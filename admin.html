🛡Admin allow-list

Emails on this list become admin automatically — if the user has already registered, they're promoted immediately; if they haven't, they're promoted at the moment of sign-up. Two sources are merged: env entries come from the PORTAL_ADMIN_EMAILS deploy config (you have to redeploy to change them), and firestore entries are added here and editable on the fly.

🧬Lab data access

Emails on this list can see the In-house data (the private cancers) on the portal — everyone else, even signed-in users, cannot. This is separate from the admin allow-list above. Two sources are merged: env entries come from the PORTAL_LAB_EMAILS deploy config, and firestore entries are added here and take effect immediately.
